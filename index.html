ClimbingLogger

A new tool for climbing training

(iPhone only for now)

For those who take it seriously...

  • Track

    Track the intensity of your sessions to make sure you are getting enough recovery

  • Plan

    Plan your training on and off the wall

  • Statistics

    Get valuable insights into statistics such as flash rate, style preference and areas of possible improvements

...those who just want to have fun...

  • Memories

    Record videos of your climbing to remember the problems you climbed

  • Cheer on

    Give your friends encouragement when they have done a session or progressed on a project

  • Project together

    Create a feed for the problem you are projecting and join forces with others who are working on the same problem

...and those who want to try something new

  • New exercises

    Find and share new exercises with the community and include them in your warm-ups or sessions

  • Heart rate

    Get insight into how your heart rate reacts to various levels of effort and how it recovers when you rest

  • Work your weaknesses

    See what kind of problems you are spending the least time on, maybe they are the key to some personal development
